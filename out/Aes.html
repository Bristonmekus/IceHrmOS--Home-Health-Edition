(static) Ctr

Aes.Ctr: Counter-mode (CTR) wrapper for AES.

This encrypts a Unicode string to produces a base64 ciphertext using 128/192/256-bit AES, and the converse to decrypt an encrypted ciphertext.

See http://csrc.nist.gov/publications/nistpubs/800-38a/sp800-38a.pdf
